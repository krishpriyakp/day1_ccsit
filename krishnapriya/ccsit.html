CCSIT, formerly known as Computer Centre, was started in 1994 with an evening MCA course. Gradually, the University started more centers in five districts under the jurisdiction of University of Calicut due to growing demands of the people and local authorities. Currently CCSIT has 13 centers outspread over five districts with various courses like MCA, MSc Computer science, BSc IT and BCA courses. These centers impart high quality education for computer aspirants. For over two decades these centers have contributed qualified and committed software engineers and academicians who excel in their respective fields.
